Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common appointment-related queries.

How can I book an appointment?

You can easily schedule your appointment online using the booking form or by calling our support team.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes. Simply contact us before your appointment time and we'll help you choose another slot.

When will I receive my reports?

Most routine diagnostic reports are available on the same day, while specialized tests may require additional processing time.

Do I need to fast before my test?

Some tests require fasting. Our team will inform you about any preparation needed when your appointment is confirmed.
